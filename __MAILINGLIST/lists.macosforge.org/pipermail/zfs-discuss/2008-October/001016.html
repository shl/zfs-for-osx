Hi Dillon,

The following command-line will give you the amount of bytes consumed by
all of your snapshots:

zfs list -t snapshot -H -o used | sed 's/K/*1024/' | sed 's/M/ 
*1024*1024/' |
sed 's/G/*1024*1024*1024/' | sed 's/^/a+=/' | awk '{ print $0 } END  
{ print "a"
}' | bc

Best regards,

Werner.
