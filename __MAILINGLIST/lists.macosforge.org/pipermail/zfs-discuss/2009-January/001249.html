You sort of can using a fifo.  Do something like:

mkfifo /tmp/blah
zfs send > /tmp/blah
zfs recv < /tmp/blah

Do the send and recv commands in different shells.

It's not perfect, but it does allow you to do a send/recv without
writing to an intermediate file.
